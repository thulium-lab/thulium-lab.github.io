Collaborators

Avesta-project

Avesta LTD develops and manufactures femtosecond laser oscillators and amplifiers (both solid-state and fiber-based) as well as ultrafast diagnostic tools and various laser components.

Learn more
Skoltech

Skolkovo Institute of Science and Technology (Skoltech) is involved in many collaborations with leading research centers in the field of optical frequency standards, including Physikalisch-Technische Bundesanstalt (PTB), Braunschweig, Germany. An original concept of optical frequency standard based on single ytterbium ions was developed in the laboratory of time and freqeuncy measuring problems.

Learn more
Institute of Laser Physics

Institute of Laser Physics of the Siberian branch of the Russian Academy of Sciences is one of the leading research centers in the field of optical frequency standards. There are several projects for creating optical frequency standards currently under way (based on neutral magnesium and ytterbium ion, relative instability is expected to be around 10-17 — 10-18).

Learn more

Industrial partner

JSC "Russian Space Systems"

The Russian Corporation for Rocket and Science Instrument Engineering and Information Systems (JSC "Russian Space Systems") is an expert in development, manufacture and operation of space information systems, including the development and targeted use of the GLONASS global navigation satellite system, space search and rescue systems, hydrometeorological support, radio engineering for space research, ground stations for receiving and processing Earth remote sensing information.
